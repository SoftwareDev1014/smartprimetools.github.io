Cancellation Policy

1. Overview

SmartPrimeTools LLC provides digital software services that may be offered on a subscription or one-time purchase basis. This Cancellation Policy outlines how customers can cancel their services.

2. Subscription Cancellation

Customers may cancel subscription-based services at any time before the next billing cycle. Cancellation requests must be made prior to the renewal date to avoid being charged for the next period.

3. Effect of Cancellation

After cancellation, customers will continue to have access to the service until the end of the current billing period. No further charges will be applied after cancellation is confirmed.

4. No Partial Refunds

Once a billing cycle has started, fees are generally non-refundable. Partial refunds for unused time are not provided unless required by applicable law.

5. One-Time Purchases

For one-time purchases of digital products, cancellations are not applicable once access or delivery has been provided.

6. Account Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate our policies or applicable laws. In such cases, no refunds may be issued.
