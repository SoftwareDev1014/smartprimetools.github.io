Refund & Dispute Policy

1. Overview

SmartPrimeTools LLC provides digital software products and services delivered electronically. Due to the nature of digital content, all sales are generally considered final once access has been granted.

2. Refund Eligibility

Customers may request a refund within 7 days of purchase. Refunds are not guaranteed and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Refunds may be approved under the following conditions:

  • Technical issues that prevent proper use of the service
  • Duplicate or accidental transactions
  • Failure of the service to perform as described

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Failure to cancel a subscription before renewal
  • Misunderstanding of service features clearly described on the website

4. Dispute Resolution

Customers are encouraged to contact us directly before initiating a dispute or chargeback. We are committed to resolving issues quickly and fairly.

Filing a dispute without prior contact may delay resolution and may result in account suspension or termination.

5. Processing of Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider.
